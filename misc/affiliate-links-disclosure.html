Affiliate Links Disclosure

This guide contains affiliate links. This means that if you click on certain links and subsequently make a purchase or booking while backpacking around South Africa, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

We believe in being entirely transparent with our readers. We only recommend products, tour operators, and services - such as those found on GetYourGuide or Booking.com - that we have personally vetted, used, or believe will add genuine value to your travels in South Africa.

Why do we use affiliate links?

Maintaining this guide involves costs related to IT and staffing. These commissions help support the guide and allow us to continue providing high-quality, free content for the backpacking community.

Our commitment to you

Honesty: Our editorial content is never influenced by affiliate partnerships. We will always provide our honest opinion, whether it is positive or negative.

Price: Using an affiliate link never increases the price you pay. In some instances, our partnerships may even allow us to offer you a unique discount code.

Privacy: Clicking an affiliate link does not give us access to your personal details or credit card information. The transaction remains private between you and the third-party vendor.

We are a participant in several affiliate programmes, designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to reputable travel service providers.
